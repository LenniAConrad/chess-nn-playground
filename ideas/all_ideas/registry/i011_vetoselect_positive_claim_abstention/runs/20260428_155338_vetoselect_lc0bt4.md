## Decision

Keep the implementation as a tested candidate, but do not treat A2 as a win. The next VetoSelect-specific step should be A3: add deterministic current-board rule texture for the decoy target, then rerun. If the goal is a new architecture rather than objective refinement, move to Soft-Dykstra or Sparse Relation Pursuit from `ideas/all_ideas/registry/TODO.md`.
