Empty-Square Opportunity Network

Working thesis: Chess tactics often depend on empty squares: escape squares, mating squares, promotion paths, discovered-attack landing squares, fork squares, and blocking/interference squares. A classifier that separately models occupied-square evidence and empty-square opportunity may capture useful signal missed by ordinary CNN pooling.
