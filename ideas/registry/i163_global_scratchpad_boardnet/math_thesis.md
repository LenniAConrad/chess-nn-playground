Global Scratchpad BoardNet

Working thesis: A board CNN can be augmented with a small recurrent global scratchpad: a fixed number of memory vectors that summarize the board, are updated a few times, and broadcast context back to squares through affine modulation. This gives global communication without attention or dense square-pair tensors.
