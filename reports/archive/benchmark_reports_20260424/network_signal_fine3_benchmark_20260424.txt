## Notes

- All four models trained for 3 epochs on the balanced `crtk_sample_3class` split: 360k train, 45k validation, 45k test.
- The BT4 model uses the current FEN-only `lc0_bt4_112` tensor; older history planes are zero in this dataset.
- The artifact validator warns about missing fine-to-binary matrices because these runs are direct 3-class runs; the standard 3x3 validation/test confusion matrices were produced.
